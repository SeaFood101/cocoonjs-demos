Setting up products

Fetch products from store

The first thing you have to do is to initialize the store service. 

Cocoon.store.initialize();

After initializing the store service, you have to fetch the products you'd set up on Google Play/App Store/Amazon Store, first setup the callbacks 

Cocoon.Store.on("load",{ 
    success: function(productInfo){ 
      console.log(productInfo.productId); 
    }, 
    error: function(){ 
      console.log("Cannot fetch products from store"); 
    } 
});

Then you can fetch the products 

Cocoon.Store.fetchProducts(["remove.ads"]);

After being notified through the Cocoon.Store.on("load",{ success : ... }); event, you can buy the product by calling the Cocoon.Store.purchaseProduct.

That's all for now.
