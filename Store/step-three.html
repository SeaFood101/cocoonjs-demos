Purchasing a product

When the purchase starts, you'll be notified through the transaction listeners.

Cocoon.Store.on("purchase",{
        started: function(){ ... },
        success: function(){ ... },
        error: function(){ ... }
});

And that's ll you need to know about how to integrate in-app purchases using CocoonJS.

Check doc.ludei.com for more information of the in-app purchases extension.
